## doc

			East  West  
	North 1 2 3 1 2 3  
			4 5 6 4 5 6  
	South 7 8 9 7 8 9  
	(0 = dummy)  
	origin = lat 90, lng 0 -> lat -90, lng 90(E) -90(W)  
divide the earth to two (West or East)  
	W5555555 = level 8  
	E1384700 = level 6  
	longer is more in detail
